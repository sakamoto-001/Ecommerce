Our Studio

Home / About

ASMIRE was founded in 2026 out of a desire to create garments that belong to no single gender. We believe that style is a language of shape, weight, and texture, rather than categorization.

Our collections emphasize structural geometry, organic cotton loops, linen grains, and muted earth tones. We construct clothing meant to layer, stack, and frame personality.

Unisex Proportions

Instead of standard grading parameters, our patterns are sketched from scratch using diverse models. We measure shoulder drapes, waist falls, and sleeve cuffs across body profiles to ensure fluid structural shapes.

Each garment carries adjustable buttons, elasticized utility waistbands, or oversized tailoring folds, making ASMIRE pieces adaptable to individuals, not numbers.

Material Consciousness

Every fiber is selected with consideration. We source certified organic GOTS cotton, natural breathability flax linens, and recycled polyester blends for outerwear shells.

By partnering with local factories that employ clean recycling loop systems, we ensure minimal water waste and chemical runoff. We produce in small-batch runs to eliminate inventory waste.
